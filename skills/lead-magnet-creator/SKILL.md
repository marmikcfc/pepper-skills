# Lead Magnet Creator

Design and write a lead magnet your ICP actually wants — mapped to their pain points and the right funnel stage.

## When to Use
- "Create a lead magnet for [ICP]"
- "We need a top-of-funnel content asset"
- "Build something that will grow our email list"
- "Design a [template/guide/tool] for [audience]"

## Prerequisites
- `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`
- ICP definition and pain points

## Workflow

**Step 1: Match format to funnel stage**

| Stage | Best format | Conversion intent |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| Awareness | Research report, study, listicle | Low — educate |
| Consideration | Comparison guide, ROI calculator, checklist | Medium — evaluate |
| Decision | Template, swipe file, free tool | High — ready to act |

**Step 2: Choose the right format**

| Format | Best for | Effort |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Checklist | Process-oriented buyers | Low |
| Template | Operational roles | Low |
| Mini-guide (5-10p) | Complex problems | Medium |
| ROI calculator | Finance/ops buyers | Medium |
| Industry benchmark | Data-hungry decision makers | High |
| Email course (5-7 days) | Nurture-heavy audiences | Medium |

**Step 3: Validate the topic**

The best lead magnets are:
- **Specific**: "Email subject lines for SaaS trials" > "Email tips"
- **Actionable**: Something to do, not just know
- **Immediately useful**: Can be applied today
- **Adjacent to your product**: Problem you solve → asset they need → your solution

**Step 4: Write the lead magnet**

```
Create a [format] lead magnet for [ICP] on: [pain point]

Title: [working title]
Audience: [role at company type]
Their pain: [in their words]
What they get: [specific takeaway]

Write the full content including: title, intro, main content, CTA.
```

**Step 5: Write landing page copy**

Headline, 3-5 benefit bullets, social proof line, form CTA, thank-you message.

**Step 6: Distribution plan**

- Organic: blog CTA, homepage banner, social
- Paid: LinkedIn targeting ICP
- Community: relevant Slack/Discord groups
- Partner: co-promote with complementary tools

## Output
Complete lead magnet content, landing page copy, and distribution plan.
