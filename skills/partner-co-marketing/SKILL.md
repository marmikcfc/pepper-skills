# Partner Co-Marketing

Design and execute a co-marketing campaign that benefits both parties — from partner identification to joint execution.

## When to Use
- "Design a co-marketing campaign with [partner]"
- "We want to partner with [company] for a campaign"
- "Find partners for joint marketing"

## Prerequisites
- `ANTHROPIC_API_KEY`

## Workflow

**Step 1: Define partnership criteria**

Ideal co-marketing partner:
- Shares your ICP but doesn't compete
- Has comparable or larger audience
- Has a distribution asset you lack
- Has a reciprocal need you can fill

**Step 2: Identify candidates**

```
Our product: [description]
Our ICP: [description]
Our primary value: [value prop]

Suggest 10 partner candidates that:
1. Share our ICP but don't compete
2. Would benefit from reaching our audience
3. Have a complementary product
4. Are in a similar growth stage
```

**Step 3: Choose the campaign type**

| Type | Best for |
|------|---------|
| Joint webinar | Both have audiences |
| Co-authored guide | Credibility play |
| Integration spotlight | Product synergy |
| Newsletter swap | Audience building |
| Referral + promotion | Commercial collaboration |

**Step 4: Write the partner brief**

```
Co-marketing brief for [partner]:
Our company: [1-liner]
Their company: [1-liner]
Shared ICP: [who we're both targeting]
Campaign: [type + description]
We bring: [audience, distribution, content]
They bring: [audience, distribution, content]
Goal: [leads / awareness / pipeline]
Success metrics: [how we measure]
Timeline: [6-week plan]
```

**Step 5: Partner outreach email**

```
Write a partner co-marketing outreach email to [partner]'s marketing team proposing [campaign type].
Under 200 words. Lead with what's in it for them.
```

**Step 6: Execution plan**

| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| W1 | Brief aligned, assets inventory |
| W2 | Assets created |
| W3 | Review and approvals |
| W4 | Teaser |
| W5 | Full launch |
| W6 | Amplification + report |

## Output
Partner criteria, campaign design, partner brief, outreach email, and 6-week execution plan.
